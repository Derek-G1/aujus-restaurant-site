What is Chicago Style Italian Beef?

A staple of Chicago cuisine, the Italian Beef sandwich originated in the 1930s. It consists of thin slices of seasoned roast beef, simmered and served in its own juices (the "au jus") on a long Italian-style roll.

At Au Jus in Parma, we serve it the authentic way: topped with your choice of Chicago-style Giardiniera (pickled vegetables) or sweet peppers. You can order it dry, wet, or dipped!

About Au Jus - Parma's Italian Beef Specialist

Welcome to Au Jus, the premier destination for Authentic Chicago Style Italian Beef in Parma, Ohio. Unlike a standard sandwich shop, we slow-roast our seasoned beef daily, serving it thinly sliced on fresh French bread and dipped in our signature homemade gravy (au jus).


Whether you are craving a "wet" beef sandwich with hot giardiniera, a spicy sausage sandwich, or need catering in Northeast Ohio for your next event, we are committed to being the area's top-rated casual dining restaurant.
